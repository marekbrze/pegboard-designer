# Pegboard Designer

Plan your pegboard layout visually before buying accessories.

**[Open the app →](https://marekbrze.github.io/pegboard-designer/)**

---

## Features

- **Multiple board sizes** — Choose from 4 standard sizes: 36×56, 56×56, 76×56, and 56×37 cm
- **Built-in accessories** — Hooks, shelves, containers, baskets, cable organizers, memo boards, and more
- **Custom elements** — Create your own accessories with custom dimensions and colors
- **Double-sided mode** — Plan layouts for both sides of a standing board
- **Drag & drop** — Easily place, move, and rearrange items on the grid
- **Item properties** — Click any placed item to see its position, size, and dimensions
- **Export & import** — Save your layout as a JSON file and load it back later
- **Auto-save** — Your work is automatically saved in your browser

---

## How to Use

1. **Select your board size** — Use the buttons at the top to match your pegboard
2. **Drag accessories** — Pick an accessory from the left panel and drag it onto the board
3. **Move items** — Drag placed items to reposition them
4. **Select items** — Click an item to view its properties in the right panel
5. **Remove items** — Select an item and click "Remove element" to delete it
6. **Create custom elements** — Click "+ Add custom element" to define your own accessories
7. **Enable double-sided** — Check "Double-sided (standing)" to plan both front and back layouts
8. **Save your work** — Click "Export JSON" to download your layout for backup or sharing

---

## Tips

- Works best on desktop browsers
- Your layout auto-saves locally — come back anytime to continue
- Export your JSON file to backup your layout or share it with others
- Use custom elements for accessories not included in the standard list
